JP Solutions – Employee & Organizational Knowledge Base Document
Version: 4.0 Last Updated: February 24, 2026

1.  Company Overview and Official Information JP Solutions, founded in
    2010 and headquartered in Bangalore, India, is a pioneer in online
    test assessment software development. Led by CEO Sam, the
    organization employs over 1,000+ professionals across engineering,
    artificial intelligence, product development, human resources,
    compliance, sales, and operations. The official registered name of
    the company is JP Solutions Pvt. Ltd., and this name must be used in
    all legal, contractual, and financial documentation.

2.  Leave Application Procedure and Permission Policy Employees must
    apply for leave through the official HRMS portal at least two
    working days in advance for planned leave. Emergency leave requires
    prior notification to the reporting manager via official company
    email before HRMS submission. The maximum short-term permission
    allowed during working hours is two hours per instance and should
    not exceed four instances per month, subject to managerial approval.
    All leave and permissions must be recorded in the HRMS system for
    payroll and attendance tracking.

3.  Employee Loan and Salary Advance Eligibility Full-time employees who
    have completed a minimum of one year of continuous service are
    eligible to apply for internal loans or salary advances. Requests
    are reviewed by the HR and Finance departments. Approved loans are
    repaid through structured monthly payroll deductions. Emergency
    salary advances may be granted under exceptional circumstances with
    leadership approval.

4.  Official Email and Communication Procedures All official
    communication must be conducted through the company-provided email
    ID in the format firstname.lastname@jpsolutions.com. Professional
    standards, clear subject lines, and official signatures are
    mandatory. Escalation hierarchy follows: Employee → Reporting
    Manager → Department Head → HR or Executive Leadership. Sensitive
    data such as assessment content or candidate information must not be
    shared via personal email or unsecured platforms.

5.  Working Hours and Attendance Policy Standard working hours are 9:30
    AM to 6:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Attendance is tracked through
    secure login or biometric systems. Remote and hybrid employees must
    connect via secure VPN access. Monthly attendance compliance is
    monitored by HR in accordance with company guidelines and Karnataka
    labor regulations.

6.  Employee Benefits and Welfare Programs JP Solutions provides
    comprehensive health insurance, accident coverage, paid time off,
    maternity and paternity benefits, and performance-based bonuses. The
    company supports professional development through certification
    reimbursements, internal training programs, leadership development
    initiatives, and technical upskilling workshops.

7.  Privacy, Security, and Regulatory Compliance JP Solutions processes
    candidate data, assessment results, AI proctoring recordings, and
    client information through secure cloud infrastructure. The company
    complies with Indian IT laws, applicable data protection
    regulations, and international standards such as GDPR where
    relevant. Security controls include encryption, multi-factor
    authentication, role-based access control, and periodic security
    audits.

8.  Metadata Framework for RAG-Based Company Bot All organizational
    documents follow a standardized metadata structure including
    Document ID, Department, Policy Category, Version, Effective Date,
    Review Date, Access Level, Tags, and Jurisdiction. Content is
    semantically structured to enable efficient chunking and embedding
    generation for accurate Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
    responses. Version control ensures the chatbot retrieves the most
    current approved policy information in real time.
